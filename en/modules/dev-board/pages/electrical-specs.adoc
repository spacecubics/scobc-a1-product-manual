== Absolute Maximum Ratings

[width="100%",cols="3,3,2,1,1,1",options="header",]
|===
|Item |Symbol |Condition |Minimum |Maximum |Unit
|Supply Voltage |5V, VIN_A_TRM, VIN_B_TRM | |-0.3 |12 |V
|FPGA I/O Bank Supply Voltage |VDD_UIO1_TRM, VDD_UIO2_TRM |When OBC is installed |-0.3 |3.6 |V
|Operating Temperature |T~opr~ |No condensation |-20 |70 |℃
|===

Absolute maximum ratings are values that must not be exceeded even momentarily under any operating or test conditions. +
Exceeding absolute maximum ratings may cause permanent damage to the device. +
Please use the device with a margin relative to the above values.
