= Precautions

== Safety Precautions

To ensure the safe use of this product, please pay particular attention to the following points:

* The SC-OBC Module A1 Development Board uses general-purpose consumer electronic components. Do not use it in applications where extremely high reliability and safety are required for function and precision, such as in aerospace, aviation, medical, nuclear, transportation, traffic, or in safety devices where human life, accidents, or significant property damage could be involved.

== Handling Precautions

Observe the following precautions when handling this product:

* Do not insert or remove connectors into interfaces other than hot-swappable ones (USB, SC Bus) while power is supplied to this product or its peripheral circuits.
* Avoid using the product in environments with continuous vibration (e.g., in vehicles) or impact, as this may shorten its lifespan and increase the likelihood of failure.
